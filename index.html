WE ARE THE

BARBER SHOP


Barber Shop established in 1957, we have brought back elements of the traditional barber where quality, not quantity is the focus, in a friendly relaxed environment. Barber Shop prides itself on being the leader of the modern-day male grooming industry. We location offers a full range hair, face treatments services tailored for men.

Our shop is conveniently located on Piccadilly, in the centre of Nottingham. Open from 10 am to 7 pm Monday to Saturdays, we are here to cater to your grooming needs.

Our team of barbers have a vast amount of skills in the barbering industry and are hand-picked for their expertise.
